Why RecyConnect?

Waste management is one of the biggest challenges of the 21st century. Most recyclable materials end up in landfills because people lack the incentive or convenient means to recycle.

RecyConnect was built to solve this by "Turning Waste into Worth." We bridge the gap between households and recycling centers by providing a transparent points-based system that rewards users for every kilogram of waste they recycle.
